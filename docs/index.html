Your phone is the keyboard.

Relay turns your Android phone into a wireless keyboard, trackpad, clipboard and file bridge for any computer — or an iPad over Bluetooth. One command to set up the desktop side.

$ curl -fsSL https://github.com/Abdk4Moura/relay-desktop/releases/latest/download/install.sh | sh
> irm https://github.com/Abdk4Moura/relay-desktop/releases/latest/download/install.ps1 | iex

Installs the receiver, enables start-on-login, and launches it. Then open the Relay app on your phone — it finds your computer automatically.

Three steps

No accounts, no pairing dances.

1 · Install

Run the one-liner on your computer (or grab the binary). It starts in the background and shows a control panel.

2 · Open the app

Launch Relay on your phone. It discovers your computer on the network — or scan its QR / enter IP + PIN.

3 · Use it

Type, swipe, scroll, copy-paste, drop files. For iPad/TV, switch the app to Bluetooth mode.
